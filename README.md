# 🎓 US Community College STEM Comparison

A data-driven comparison of affordable STEM pathways in the United States for Indian students.

---

## 📊 Overview

This project analyzes:

* Tuition costs (in-state vs out-of-state)
* Top community colleges
* STEM degree options
* Online program availability
* Living costs
* Transfer pathways to universities

---

## 🏆 Key Insights

* **California** → Lowest tuition + strongest transfer system
* **Texas** → Best balance (cost + online + Indian community)
* **North Carolina** → #1 for online programs
* **New Mexico** → Cheapest overall (free tuition programs)

## 🔁 Transfer Pathways

Students can transfer to:

* UC Berkeley, UCLA
* UT Austin, Texas A&M
* Rutgers University
* University of Washington

---

## 💡 Key Takeaways

* Community colleges offer **low-cost entry into US education**
* After 1 year, students qualify for **in-state tuition**
* STEM lab courses require **in-person attendance**
* Choosing the right state significantly affects total cost
